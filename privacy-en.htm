Privacy Policy for YNPixel

Last updated:04/13/2026

At YNStudios, we respect your privacy. This policy describes how YNPixel handles data in our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

YNPixel is a local creation tool. We do not require account registration. We do not collect or store your name, email, or personal identity information on our servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of YNPixel uses Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (Android Advertising ID) to show personalized ads and analyze ad performance. You can reset these identifiers in your Android device settings.

3. In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

Premium version payments are handled exclusively by Google Play. YNStudios does not have access to your financial data or credit cards.

4. Storage Permissions

The app requests storage access only to save, export, or import your pixel art files (.ynpixel, PNG, GIF). We do not access your private photos or personal files.

5. Data Deletion

Since we don't store user data on servers, "data deletion" is achieved by uninstalling the app from your device.

6. Contact
